# Email Services at NIT Calicut

## Overview
National Institute of Technology Calicut (NIT Calicut) provides official email accounts to its students, faculty, and staff. These accounts serve as the primary medium for official communication within the institute and are integral to accessing various institutional resources. The email service operates under the `@nitc.ac.in` domain.

## Details
The email services at NIT Calicut are powered by **Google Workspace for Education**. This platform provides users with a suite of communication and collaboration tools in addition to email.

*   **Platform:** Google Workspace for Education
*   **Domain:** `@nitc.ac.in`
*   **Access:** Users can access their email through the standard Gmail web interface (mail.google.com) or configure email clients using standard protocols such as IMAP and POP3.
*   **Features:** As part of Google Workspace for Education, accounts typically include access to Gmail, Google Drive, Google Calendar, Google Meet, and other associated Google services, subject to the specific configuration and policies set by NIT Calicut.
*   **Storage:** Information on specific storage quotas for individual accounts is not publicly detailed by NIT Calicut. Storage allocations generally follow the policies and limits provided by Google Workspace for Education, which can vary.

## Facilities
The overall Information Technology (IT) infrastructure and the management of email services at NIT Calicut are overseen by the **Computer Centre (CC)**. The Computer Centre is responsible for the provisioning, maintenance, and support of the institute's network and computing resources, including the institutional email system.
