# Technology Services at NIT Calicut

## Overview

The Computer Centre at NIT Calicut serves as the central facility responsible for managing the institute's computing and networking infrastructure. It provides essential IT services to students, faculty, staff, and administrative departments, ensuring seamless operation of academic, research, and administrative activities across the campus.

Key services provided by the Computer Centre include campus-wide internet connectivity (both wired and wireless), official email services, website hosting and maintenance, general software support, and the management of central servers and network infrastructure.

## Details

The Computer Centre is responsible for the provision and maintenance of various technology services:

*   **Network and Internet Connectivity:** The Centre maintains a high-speed, campus-wide network infrastructure, offering both wired and wireless internet access across academic blocks, administrative buildings, and student hostels.
*   **Email Services:** Official email accounts are provided to students, faculty, and staff for institutional communication and access to various online resources.
*   **Website Management:** The Centre is responsible for hosting, maintaining, and ensuring the security and accessibility of the official National Institute of Technology Calicut website.
*   **Software Support:** It provides general software support and manages various software licenses essential for academic and research purposes across different departments.
*   **Server and Infrastructure Management:** The Centre oversees the operation, maintenance, and security of central servers, network devices, and other critical IT infrastructure components that support institutional services.
*   **Support for Departments:** It offers IT support, consultation, and technical assistance to various academic and administrative departments within the institute for their specific computing needs.

## Facilities

The Computer Centre manages and supports several key facilities:

*   **Computer Labs:** The Centre maintains general-purpose computer labs accessible to students for academic coursework, assignments, and general computing needs. It also provides support for specialized labs located in various academic departments, equipped with specific hardware and software as required by their respective curricula and research areas.
*   **Central Servers:** A robust server infrastructure is maintained to host various institutional services, including the official website, email services, and other critical applications.
*   **Network Infrastructure:** The campus network comprises a backbone of fiber optics, switches, routers, and numerous Wi-Fi access points strategically placed to ensure ubiquitous and reliable connectivity throughout the campus.
*   **Software Resources:** The Centre facilitates access to and manages licenses for a range of essential software packages utilized for teaching, learning, and research activities across different disciplines within the institute.
