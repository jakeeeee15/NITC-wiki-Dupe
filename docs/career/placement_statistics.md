## Procedures

The placement process at NIT Calicut is systematically organized by the Training & Placement Department to ensure a smooth and efficient interaction between students and recruiters. The general procedure typically follows these steps:

**Explanation of the Procedure:**

1.  **Student Registration:** Eligible students register with the T&P Department, providing their academic details, resumes, and other relevant information.
2.  **Company Invitation & Pre-Placement Talks (PPTs):** The T&P Department invites companies to participate in the placement drive. Companies often conduct PPTs on campus (or virtually) to introduce themselves, their work culture, and the roles they are offering.
3.  **Student Application & Resume Submission:** Interested students apply for specific roles through the T&P portal, submitting their resumes.
4.  **Shortlisting by Company:** Companies review the applications and resumes and shortlist candidates based on their criteria (e.g., academic performance, skills, projects).
5.  **Assessment Rounds:** Shortlisted candidates undergo various assessment stages, which may include online aptitude tests, technical tests, and group discussions.
6.  **Interviews:** Candidates who clear the assessment rounds proceed to interview stages, typically involving technical interviews (to assess domain knowledge) and HR interviews (to assess soft skills, cultural fit, and motivation).
7.  **Offer Letters Issued:** Companies extend job offers to selected candidates.
8.  **Student Acceptance/Rejection:** Students review the offer letters and decide whether to accept or reject the offer within a stipulated timeframe.
9.  **Placement Concluded:** Upon acceptance, the placement process for that student with that company is concluded.

The T&P Department acts as a liaison between students and companies, managing schedules, logistics, and communication throughout the entire process.
