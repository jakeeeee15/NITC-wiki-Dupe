# Getting Started at NIT Calicut

## Overview

This wiki page provides an introductory guide for new students joining the National Institute of Technology Calicut (NIT Calicut). It outlines the general processes and key considerations for admission, registration, and initial campus integration. Prospective and admitted students are strongly advised to consult the official NIT Calicut website and relevant admission portals for the most current, detailed, and specific instructions, as procedures and schedules are subject to change annually.

## Details

### Admission Process
Admission to undergraduate programs at NIT Calicut is primarily conducted through the Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) counseling process, based on the ranks obtained in the JEE (Main) examination. Postgraduate admissions typically involve national-level entrance examinations such as GATE (for M.Tech), NIMCET (for MCA), or other specific entrance tests, followed by centralized counseling or institute-level procedures.

### Document Verification
Upon provisional admission, students are required to undergo a document verification process. This typically involves submitting and verifying original academic certificates, identity proofs, category certificates (if applicable), medical certificates, and other relevant documents as specified in the admission brochure. Both online and physical verification stages may be part of the process.

### Fee Payment
Students are required to pay various fees, which generally include tuition fees, hostel fees, mess charges, and other institutional fees. The specific fee structure and payment deadlines are communicated through official admission notices and the institute's website.

### Academic Registration
Following successful document verification and fee payment, students must complete their academic registration. This process involves formally enrolling in courses, obtaining a student ID, and receiving information regarding academic calendars, timetables, and departmental affiliations.

### Hostel Allotment
NIT Calicut provides hostel accommodation for its students. The hostel allotment process is typically managed by the Dean (Students' Welfare) office or a dedicated hostel administration. Details regarding application procedures, allotment criteria, and hostel rules are usually published on the institute's website prior to the commencement of the academic session.

### Orientation Program
An orientation program is generally conducted for new students to familiarize them with the institute's academic environment, campus facilities, rules and regulations, and various student support services. Specific schedules and activities for the orientation program are announced annually.

## History

NIT Calicut was established in 1961 as one of the Regional Engineering Colleges (RECs) set up by the Government of India. It was initially known as Regional Engineering College Calicut (RECC). In 2002, the institution was elevated to the status of a National Institute of Technology (NIT) and was granted deemed university status, becoming NIT Calicut. It was subsequently declared an Institute of National Importance under the National Institutes of Technology Act, 2007.

## Facilities

NIT Calicut provides a range of facilities to support its students' academic and residential needs. These generally include:

*   **Academic Infrastructure:** Departmental buildings, lecture halls, laboratories, workshops, and computer centers.
*   **Library:** A central library providing access to a collection of books, journals, periodicals, and digital resources.
*   **Hostels:** Separate hostel facilities for male and female students, typically equipped with basic amenities.
*   **Mess Facilities:** Centralized mess services are usually available within or near the hostel complexes.
*   **Medical Centre:** A health center or medical unit to provide basic medical care to students and staff.
*   **Sports and Recreation:** Facilities for various indoor and outdoor sports, gymnasium, and recreational areas.
*   **Other Amenities:** Canteens, banking services, postal services, and transportation facilities within the campus.

Specific details regarding the names, capacities, and operational procedures of these facilities are available on the official NIT Calicut website.

## Procedures

### Undergraduate Admission and Enrollment Flow

The general flow for undergraduate admission and enrollment at NIT Calicut through the JoSAA process is outlined below:

```mermaid
graph TD
    A[JEE (Main) Examination] --> B{JoSAA Counseling & Seat Allotment};
    B -- Allotted Seat --> C[Provisional Admission & Fee Payment (JoSAA Portal)];
    C --> D{Reporting to NIT Calicut (Online/Physical Document Verification)};
    D -- Verification Successful --> E[Final Admission & Fee Payment (NIT Calicut Portal)];
    E --> F[Hostel Allotment Application & Confirmation];
    F --> G[Academic Registration & Course Enrollment];
    G --> H[Orientation Program Participation];
    H --> I[Commencement of Classes];
```

### Academic Registration Process

The academic registration process typically involves the following steps, though specific details may vary each year:

```mermaid
graph TD
    A[Receive Admission Offer] --> B{Complete Fee Payment};
    B --> C[Online Registration Portal Access];
    C --> D[Fill Personal & Academic Details];
    D --> E[Upload Required Documents (Scanned Copies)];
    E --> F[Departmental/Faculty Approval];
    F -- Approved --> G[Receive Student ID & Roll Number];
    G --> H[Collect Student ID Card & Academic Materials];
    H --> I[Access Course Timetable & Learning Management System];
```
