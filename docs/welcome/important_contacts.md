# Important Contacts at NIT Calicut

## Overview

This page serves as a guide for students of the National Institute of Technology Calicut (NITC) to identify and locate important administrative and support contacts within the institute. Effective communication with relevant offices and personnel is crucial for addressing academic, administrative, and personal concerns. While specific individuals holding positions may change over time, the roles and the offices they represent remain consistent. Students are advised to always refer to the official NIT Calicut website for the most current contact details, including names, email addresses, and phone numbers of office holders.

## Details

Important contacts at NIT Calicut are typically categorized by their functional areas to facilitate student access to appropriate support.

### General Administration

These offices manage the overall functioning of the institute and provide high-level support and policy guidance.

*   **Director's Office:** The Director is the chief executive and academic officer of the institute. The office oversees all strategic decisions and overall administration.
    *   *Function:* Strategic leadership, overall policy, external relations.
    *   *Contact Information:* General contact details for the Director's office are typically available on the 'Administration' section of the official NITC website.
*   **Registrar's Office:** The Registrar is responsible for general administration, academic affairs, examinations, student records, and official communications.
    *   *Function:* Academic administration, admissions, examinations, student registration, official records, personnel matters.
    *   *Contact Information:* Contact details for the Registrar and the office are usually found under the 'Administration' or 'Contact Us' sections of the official NITC website.
*   **Deans' Offices:** NIT Calicut has various Deans overseeing specific functional areas.
    *   **Dean Academic Affairs (DAA):** Manages academic programs, curriculum development, and academic regulations.
        *   *Function:* Academic policies, course registration, academic calendar, student performance.
        *   *Contact Information:* Available on the 'Academics' or 'Administration' section of the official NITC website.
    *   **Dean Student Welfare (DSW):** Oversees student well-being, hostel administration, extracurricular activities, and student grievances.
        *   *Function:* Student support, hostel management, cultural and technical clubs, counseling services.
        *   *Contact Information:* Typically found under the 'Student Affairs' or 'Student Welfare' section of the official NITC website.
    *   **Dean Planning & Development (DPD):** Focuses on institutional planning, infrastructure development, and resource allocation.
        *   *Function:* Strategic planning, infrastructure projects, institutional development.
        *   *Contact Information:* Available on the 'Administration' section of the official NITC website.
    *   **Dean Faculty Welfare (DFW):** Addresses matters related to faculty welfare and development.
        *   *Function:* Faculty recruitment, professional development, welfare schemes.
        *   *Contact Information:* Available on the 'Administration' section of the official NITC website.

### Academic Departments

Each academic department is headed by a Head of Department (HoD) and has administrative staff to assist students with course-specific queries.

*   **Head of Department (HoD):** The HoD is responsible for the academic and administrative functioning of their respective department.
    *   *Function:* Departmental academic matters, course scheduling, faculty assignments, student academic advising within the department.
    *   *Contact Information:* HoD details and departmental office contacts are typically listed on the individual department pages on the official NITC website.

### Student Support Services

These services are dedicated to supporting the holistic development and well-being of students.

*   **Hostel Administration:** Managed by the Chief Warden and individual Hostel Wardens.
    *   *Function:* Hostel allocation, maintenance, discipline, and resident welfare.
    *   *Contact Information:* Details for the Chief Warden and individual Hostel Wardens are usually found under the 'Student Affairs' or 'Hostels' section of the official NITC website.
*   **Medical Centre:** Provides primary healthcare services to students and staff.
    *   *Function:* Medical consultations, first aid, emergency care, health awareness programs.
    *   *Contact Information:* For appointments or emergencies, contact details are typically listed on the 'Medical Centre' page of the official NITC website.
*   **Counselling Services:** Offers psychological support and guidance to students.
    *   *Function:* Mental health support, stress management, academic counseling, personal development.
    *   *Contact Information:* Details for counselors and appointment procedures are usually available on the 'Student Welfare' or 'Counselling' section of the official NITC website.
*   **Central Library:** Provides access to academic resources, including books, journals, and digital databases.
    *   *Function:* Lending services, reference services, digital resources access, study spaces.
    *   *Contact Information:* Available on the 'Library' section of the official NITC website.

### Emergency Services

For immediate assistance in critical situations.

*   **Security Office:** Responsible for campus safety and security.
    *   *Function:* Campus patrolling, access control, emergency response, incident reporting.
    *   *Contact Information:* Emergency security contact numbers are typically displayed prominently on campus notice boards and on the 'Contact Us' or 'Security' section of the official NITC website.
*   **Medical Emergency:** For urgent medical situations, the Medical Centre or designated emergency contacts should be utilized.
    *   *Function:* Immediate medical attention.
    *   *Contact Information:* Emergency medical contact numbers are usually available on the 'Medical Centre' page and through the Security Office.

## History

The concept of "Important Contacts" at NIT Calicut has evolved with the growth of the institution since its establishment as Calicut Regional Engineering College (CREC) in 1961 and its upgrade to NIT status in 2202. The administrative structure and the roles of key personnel have been adapted over time to meet the increasing needs of a larger student body and expanding academic programs. While the specific individuals in these roles have changed, the fundamental functions of these offices in supporting students have remained consistent.

## Procedures

Students often need to follow specific procedures to address their concerns or seek information. The general flow for common student interactions is outlined below. Specific, detailed procedures for various academic and administrative processes are published on the official NIT Calicut website and departmental notice boards.

### General Information Seeking Procedure

```mermaid
graph TD
    A[Student] --> B{Query/Information Need};
    B --> C[Consult Official NITC Website];
    C -- Information Found --> D[Self-Resolution];
    C -- Information Not Found --> E{Identify Relevant Office/Department};
    E --> F[Locate Contact Information (Website/Notice Board)];
    F --> G[Initiate Contact (Email/Phone/Visit)];
    G --> H{Resolution/Further Guidance};
```

### Academic Grievance Redressal Procedure (General)

For academic issues such as grading, course content, or faculty interaction, a hierarchical approach is generally followed.

```mermaid
graph TD
    A[Student] --> B{Academic Grievance};
    B --> C[Contact Course Instructor];
    C -- No Resolution --> D[Contact Head of Department (HoD)];
    D -- No Resolution --> E[Contact Dean Academic Affairs (DAA)];
    E -- No Resolution --> F[Registrar / Academic Council (if applicable)];
```

### Hostel Issue Resolution Procedure (General)

For issues related to hostel facilities, discipline, or welfare.

```mermaid
graph TD
    A[Student] --> B{Hostel Issue};
    B --> C[Contact Hostel Warden / Caretaker];
    C -- No Resolution --> D[Contact Chief Warden];
    D -- No Resolution --> E[Contact Dean Student Welfare (DSW)];
```
