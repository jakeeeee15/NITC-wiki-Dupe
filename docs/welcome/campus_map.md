# Campus Map of NIT Calicut

## Overview
The Campus Map of the National Institute of Technology Calicut (NIT Calicut) is a navigational tool designed to assist students, faculty, staff, and visitors in locating various buildings, facilities, and points of interest within the institute's campus. It provides a visual representation of the campus layout, aiding in orientation and efficient movement across the extensive grounds.

## Details
The campus map typically illustrates the geographical arrangement of NIT Calicut's infrastructure. Key elements commonly depicted on the map include:
*   **Academic Blocks:** Buildings housing various engineering departments, lecture halls, and laboratories.
*   **Administrative Block:** Central offices for institute administration, including the Director's office, Registrar's office, and other administrative sections.
*   **Central Library:** The main library facility.
*   **Computer Centre:** Centralized computing facilities.
*   **Hostels:** Residential blocks for male and female students, often individually named or numbered.
*   **Health Centre:** On-campus medical facility.
*   **Sports Complex:** Areas dedicated to sports and recreational activities, including the main stadium, indoor stadium, and various courts.
*   **Guest House:** Accommodation for official guests and visitors.
*   **Staff Quarters:** Residential areas for faculty and staff.
*   **Dining Facilities:** Canteens and food courts.
*   **Essential Services:** Locations of facilities such as banks, post office, and cooperative stores.
*   **Road Network:** Internal roads, pathways, and parking areas.
*   **Entry/Exit Points:** Main gates and other access points to the campus.

The map is generally available in static formats, such as downloadable PDF documents or image files, accessible via the official NIT Calicut website. Physical signboards with campus maps may also be present at strategic locations on campus.

## Facilities
The campus map serves as a primary resource for students and visitors to locate the diverse range of facilities available at NIT Calicut. It enables users to identify the precise locations of:
*   **Academic Departments:** For attending classes, practical sessions, and meeting faculty.
*   **Residential Areas:** Student hostels and staff quarters.
*   **Support Services:** The Central Library, Computer Centre, Health Centre, and administrative offices.
*   **Recreational Facilities:** Sports grounds, indoor stadiums, and other areas for extracurricular activities.
*   **Commercial and Utility Services:** Banks, post office, and various canteens.
