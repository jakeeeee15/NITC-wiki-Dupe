# Airport Near NIT Calicut

## Overview

Calicut International Airport (IATA: CCJ, ICAO: VOCL), also known as Karipur Airport, is the primary air gateway serving the Malabar region of Kerala, India. It is the closest international airport to the National Institute of Technology Calicut (NIT Calicut). The airport facilitates both domestic and international air travel, primarily connecting to major Indian cities and destinations in the Middle East.

## Details

*   **Location:** Karipur, Malappuram district, Kerala, India.
*   **Distance from NIT Calicut:** Approximately 35-40 kilometers by road.
*   **Travel Time:** The typical travel time between NIT Calicut and Calicut International Airport ranges from 1 to 1.5 hours, subject to traffic conditions.
*   **Airlines:** The airport hosts a range of domestic carriers, including IndiGo, SpiceJet, and Air India. International operations are primarily handled by airlines such as Emirates, Qatar Airways, Air Arabia, Oman Air, and Saudia, among others.
*   **Destinations:** Domestic flights connect Calicut to major Indian metropolitan areas (e.g., Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad). International services predominantly link to cities within the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries.

## History

Calicut International Airport was officially inaugurated on April 13, 1988. It was subsequently granted international airport status on February 2, 2006, enabling it to handle direct international flights. Over its operational history, the airport has undergone various infrastructure developments and faced operational adjustments, including temporary restrictions on wide-body aircraft due to runway safety concerns. These restrictions were later addressed through remedial measures and operational protocols.

## Facilities

*   **Terminal:** The airport features an integrated terminal building designed to manage both domestic and international passenger traffic.
*   **Runway:** It operates with a single runway (designated 10/28).
*   **Cargo:** A dedicated cargo terminal is available for air freight operations.
*   **Ground Transportation:**
    *   **Taxis:** Pre-paid taxi services and app-based ride-hailing options are available outside the terminal.
    *   **Buses:** Public and private bus services connect the airport to Kozhikode city and surrounding areas.
    *   **Car Rentals:** Car rental services are provided for passengers.
*   **Parking:** Designated areas for both short-term and long-term vehicle parking are available.
*   **Amenities:** Passenger amenities include food and beverage outlets, retail shops, duty-free stores, currency exchange counters, and passenger lounges.
