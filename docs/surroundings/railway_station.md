# Railway Station Near NIT Calicut

## Overview

The primary railway station serving students and staff of the National Institute of Technology Calicut (NIT Calicut) is **Kozhikode Railway Station (CLT)**. Located in the city of Kozhikode (formerly Calicut), it is a major railway hub in North Kerala and provides connectivity to various parts of India. While other smaller stations may exist closer in terms of direct distance, Kozhikode Railway Station is the most significant in terms of train services, facilities, and connectivity, making it the practical choice for long-distance travel.

NIT Calicut is situated in Chathamangalam, approximately 20-25 kilometers from Kozhikode Railway Station. Travel between the institute and the station is typically facilitated by local buses, taxis, or auto-rickshaws.

## Details

*   **Station Code:** CLT
*   **Railway Zone:** Southern Railway
*   **Railway Division:** Palakkad
*   **Number of Platforms:** Approximately 4
*   **Track Gauge:** Broad Gauge
*   **Station Category:** A-1 (as per Indian Railways classification, indicating high revenue and passenger footfall)
*   **Connectivity:** Kozhikode Railway Station is a significant stop for numerous express, superfast, and passenger trains. It connects Kozhikode to major cities across India, including Chennai, Bengaluru, Mumbai, Delhi, Hyderabad, Thiruvananthapuram, Mangaluru, and Coimbatore.

## History

The railway line connecting Tirur to Kozhikode was inaugurated on 2 January 1888, marking the arrival of the railway system in Kozhikode. This line was an extension of the Beypore-Tirur line, which was one of the earliest railway lines in British India, opened in 1861. Kozhikode Railway Station has since grown to become a crucial transportation node in the Malabar region, facilitating trade, commerce, and passenger movement for over a century.

## Facilities

Kozhikode Railway Station offers a range of facilities for passengers:

*   **Ticketing:** Unreserved Ticketing System (UTS) counters for general tickets and dedicated Passenger Reservation System (PRS) counters for reserved tickets. Automatic Ticket Vending Machines (ATVMs) are also available.
*   **Waiting Rooms:** General waiting rooms and separate waiting rooms for ladies and upper-class passengers.
*   **Retiring Rooms:** Accommodation facilities for passengers, subject to availability and booking.
*   **Food and Beverages:** Multiple food stalls, refreshment rooms, and vending machines.
*   **Restrooms:** Public toilet facilities are available on platforms and in the concourse area.
*   **Cloakroom:** A facility to store luggage for a specified period, subject to railway rules.
*   **ATMs:** Automated Teller Machines from various banks are present within the station premises.
*   **Book Stalls:** Outlets selling newspapers, magazines, and books.
*   **Parking:** Designated areas for two-wheelers and four-wheelers.
*   **Accessibility:** Ramps and wheelchairs are generally available for passengers with reduced mobility.

## Procedures

### Ticket Booking Process

Students can book railway tickets through both online and offline channels.

```mermaid
graph TD
    A[Start] --> B{Choose Booking Method};
    B -- Online --> C[IRCTC Website/App];
    B -- Offline --> D[Railway Station Counter];

    C --> C1[Search Train & Availability];
    C1 --> C2[Select Train, Class & Berth Preference];
    C2 --> C3[Enter Passenger Details];
    C3 --> C4[Make Payment Online];
    C4 --> C5[Receive E-Ticket/SMS Confirmation];

    D --> D1[Approach UTS/PRS Counter];
    D1 --> D2[Fill Reservation Form (for reserved) / State Destination (for unreserved)];
    D2 --> D3[Make Payment];
    D3 --> D4[Receive Physical Ticket];

    C5 --> E[End];
    D4 --> E;
```

**Online Booking:**
*   **IRCTC Website/App:** The official platform for Indian Railways ticket booking. Requires registration and payment via various digital methods. E-tickets are generated and sent via email/SMS.

**Offline Booking:**
*   **Reservation Counters (PRS):** For reserved tickets (sleeper, AC classes). Passengers need to fill out a reservation form with train details, passenger information, and class preference.
*   **Unreserved Ticketing System (UTS) Counters:** For unreserved general class tickets and platform tickets.
*   **Automatic Ticket Vending Machines (ATVMs):** Available for purchasing unreserved tickets and renewing season tickets using smart cards or UPI.

### Platform Access

*   **Valid Ticket:** Passengers require a valid train ticket (reserved or unreserved) to access the platforms.
*   **Platform Ticket:** Individuals not travelling but accompanying passengers to the platform or seeing them off must purchase a platform ticket.
*   **Entry Points:** Access to platforms is typically through designated entry gates, often monitored by railway staff.
*   **Information Displays:** Digital display boards and announcements provide information on train arrivals, departures, and platform numbers.
