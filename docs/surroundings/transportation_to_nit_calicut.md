# Transportation to NIT Calicut

## Overview
National Institute of Technology Calicut (NITC) is situated in Kunnamangalam, approximately 22 kilometers northeast of Kozhikode (Calicut) city center in Kerala, India. The campus is accessible via various modes of transportation from major transit hubs including the Kozhikode Railway Station, Kozhikode KSRTC Bus Stand, and Calicut International Airport. The primary modes of transport available to students and visitors include public buses, auto-rickshaws, and app-based taxi services.

## Details

### From Calicut International Airport (CCJ) / Karipur Airport
Calicut International Airport (CCJ) is the nearest airport, located approximately 28-30 kilometers from the NIT Calicut campus.

*   **Taxi/Cab Services:**
    *   Pre-paid taxi counters are available at the airport terminal.
    *   App-based taxi services (e.g., Uber, Ola) operate in the region and can be booked via their respective mobile applications.
    *   Travel time typically ranges from 45 minutes to 1 hour, depending on traffic conditions.
*   **Bus Services:**
    *   Buses are available from the airport to Kozhikode city's main bus stands (Mavoor Road or Palayam).
    *   From Kozhikode city, onward local bus services are available to Kunnamangalam and the NIT Calicut campus gate. This option involves a transfer and generally takes longer.

### From Kozhikode Railway Station (CLT)
Kozhikode Railway Station (CLT) is the main railway hub, located approximately 22 kilometers from the NIT Calicut campus.

*   **Auto-rickshaws:**
    *   Auto-rickshaws are readily available outside the railway station. It is advisable to negotiate the fare or ensure the meter is used.
    *   Travel time is typically 40-50 minutes.
*   **Taxi/Cab Services:**
    *   App-based taxi services (e.g., Uber, Ola) can be booked from the railway station.
    *   Metered taxis may also be available.
    *   Travel time is typically 40-50 minutes.
*   **Bus Services:**
    *   From the railway station, one can take an auto-rickshaw or walk a short distance to the nearby Palayam or Mavoor Road KSRTC Bus Stands.
    *   Frequent local buses (both KSRTC and private) are available from these bus stands to Kunnamangalam and the NIT Calicut campus gate.
    *   Travel time by bus, including potential waiting time, can be 1 hour or more.

### From Kozhikode KSRTC Bus Stand (Mavoor Road / Palayam)
The main KSRTC (Kerala State Road Transport Corporation) bus stands in Kozhikode city are located on Mavoor Road and at Palayam. Both are approximately 22 kilometers from NIT Calicut.

*   **Local Bus Services:**
    *   Frequent local buses, both KSRTC and private, operate services to Kunnamangalam and specifically to the NIT Calicut campus gate.
    *   Students should look for buses displaying "NIT Calicut," "Kunnamangalam," or "Mukkam" as their destination.
    *   Travel time is typically 45 minutes to 1 hour.
*   **Auto-rickshaws/Taxis:**
    *   Auto-rickshaws and app-based taxis are available from the bus stands for direct travel to the campus.

### Local Transportation to Campus
The NIT Calicut campus gate is a recognized stop for public buses. From the campus gate, the academic and residential areas are accessible by walking. Auto-rickshaws are often available near the campus gate for travel within the local area or to the main campus buildings, though internal campus transport for students is not typically provided by the institute.

## History
Specific historical details regarding the evolution of transportation *to* NIT Calicut as a dedicated subject are not widely documented in public sources. However, the accessibility of the institute has generally improved with the development of the road network in the Kozhikode district, particularly the National Highway 766 (formerly NH 212) which passes near Kunnamangalam. The growth of public and private bus services, along with the introduction of app-based taxi services, has enhanced connectivity over time.

## Facilities
*   **Parking:** Limited parking facilities are available on campus for private vehicles. Policies regarding student-owned vehicles and parking access are subject to institute regulations and may require specific permits.
*   **Bus Stops:** Designated bus stops are located at the main entrance of the NIT Calicut campus, serving both KSRTC and private bus routes.
*   **Auto-rickshaw Stand:** Auto-rickshaws are typically available near the campus main gate, particularly during peak hours, for local travel.

## Procedures
*   **Bus Travel:** Bus tickets are generally purchased from the conductor on board. It is advisable to inform the conductor of the destination (NIT Calicut or Kunnamangalam) upon boarding.
*   **Taxi/Auto-rickshaw Booking:** For app-based services (e.g., Uber, Ola), booking is done through their respective mobile applications, requiring an internet connection. For traditional auto-rickshaws, fares should ideally be agreed upon before commencing the journey or the driver should be requested to use the meter.
*   **Campus Entry:** All vehicles entering the NIT Calicut campus are subject to security checks. Visitors and non-campus residents may be required to register at the security gate.
