# Central Library of NIT Calicut

## Overview

The Central Library of the National Institute of Technology Calicut (NIT Calicut) serves as a central resource hub for academic and research activities within the institute. It aims to support the educational and research needs of students, faculty, and staff by providing access to a wide range of information resources, both in print and digital formats. The library plays a crucial role in facilitating learning, research, and intellectual development across various disciplines offered at NIT Calicut.

## Details

The Central Library is located within the NIT Calicut campus. It houses a collection designed to cater to the diverse academic programs, including undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral studies, across engineering, science, and humanities disciplines.

Key aspects of the library's collection and infrastructure include:

*   **Collection:** The library maintains a collection of books, journals, periodicals, reference materials, and electronic resources. The specific number of volumes, journal subscriptions, and e-resources is subject to change and is best verified through the official library website.
*   **Electronic Resources:** Access is provided to various online databases, e-journals, e-books, and other digital content, typically accessible within the campus network or via remote access services for authorized users.
*   **Working Hours:** Specific operating hours for different sections (e.g., circulation, reading rooms) may vary and are subject to change. Users are advised to consult the official library website or notices for the most current schedule.
*   **Location:** The library building is centrally located on the NIT Calicut campus.

## Facilities

The Central Library provides a range of facilities designed to support study, research, and information access:

*   **Reading Rooms:** Dedicated spaces for individual study and reading.
*   **Circulation Section:** For borrowing and returning physical books.
*   **Reference Section:** Houses non-circulating reference materials such such as encyclopedias, dictionaries, and handbooks.
*   **Periodical Section:** Provides access to current and back issues of print journals and magazines.
*   **Digital Library/Computer Section:** Equipped with computers for accessing electronic resources, databases, and the internet.
*   **Reprography Services:** Facilities for photocopying and printing may be available.
*   **Wi-Fi Access:** Wireless internet connectivity is typically available within the library premises for registered users.

Specific details regarding the number of seats, computers, or specialized labs are not consistently published and may vary.

## Procedures

Access to and utilization of the Central Library's resources and facilities are governed by specific procedures and rules established by the institute. These generally cover membership, borrowing, renewals, and conduct within the library premises.

### Membership

Membership to the Central Library is typically granted to:

*   Students (undergraduate, postgraduate, PhD)
*   Faculty members
*   Research scholars
*   Non-teaching staff of NIT Calicut

The process usually involves registration with the library using a valid institute ID card.

### Borrowing and Returns

*   **Overdue Fines:** Fines are usually levied for books returned after their due date. Specific fine amounts are subject to library policy.
*   **Lost/Damaged Books:** Users are generally responsible for lost or damaged books and may be required to replace them or pay a penalty as per library rules.
