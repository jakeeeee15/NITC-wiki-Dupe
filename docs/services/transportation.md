# Transportation at NIT Calicut

## Overview

NIT Calicut (National Institute of Technology Calicut) is situated in Chathamangalam, approximately 22 kilometers northeast of Kozhikode (Calicut) city center, Kerala. The campus is spread over an area of about 120 hectares (297 acres). Given its location away from the immediate city center and its expansive campus size, transportation plays a role in connectivity to the institute and movement within its premises for students, faculty, and staff.

The primary modes of transportation for reaching the campus involve public and private services from Kozhikode city, its railway station, and the Calicut International Airport. Internal transportation largely relies on walking, cycling, and personal vehicles, though specific details regarding internal campus transport services are not extensively documented in publicly available sources.

## Details

### External Connectivity

NIT Calicut is accessible via road from various key locations in Kozhikode.

*   **From Kozhikode Railway Station:** The railway station is a major hub. Students can avail of public buses, private buses, or taxis to reach the campus.
*   **From Kozhikode City Bus Stand (Palayam/Mavoor Road):** Regular bus services operate from the city to the campus vicinity.
*   **From Calicut International Airport (Karipur - CCJ):** The airport is approximately 45 kilometers from the campus. Taxis and pre-booked cabs are common options. Public bus services are also available from the airport to Kozhikode city, from where onward connections to NIT Calicut can be made.

**Public Bus Services:**
Kerala State Road Transport Corporation (KSRTC) buses and private buses frequently ply the route connecting Kozhikode city to areas like Mukkam and Areekode, passing by the NIT Calicut main gate. The bus stop for NIT Calicut is located directly outside the main entrance.

**Taxi/Cab Services:**
App-based taxi services and local taxi stands are available at the railway station, airport, and various points in Kozhikode city for direct travel to the campus.
