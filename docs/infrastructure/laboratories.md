# Laboratories at NIT Calicut

## Overview
Laboratories at the National Institute of Technology Calicut (NIT Calicut) serve as fundamental infrastructure for academic instruction, practical skill development, and research activities across various engineering and science disciplines. As a premier technical institution, NIT Calicut maintains a range of specialized laboratories designed to support its undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. These facilities enable students and researchers to conduct experiments, develop prototypes, analyze data, and engage in hands-on learning experiences essential for their respective fields.

## Details
