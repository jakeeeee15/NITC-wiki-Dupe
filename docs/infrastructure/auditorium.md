# Auditorium at NIT Calicut

## Overview

The Auditorium at the National Institute of Technology Calicut (NIT Calicut) serves as the primary venue for large-scale institutional events, cultural programs, technical symposiums, and academic gatherings. It is a central facility on the campus, designed to accommodate a significant number of attendees for various functions throughout the academic year.

## Details

The main Auditorium at NIT Calicut is generally referred to as "The Auditorium" or "Main Auditorium" within the institute.

*   **Seating Capacity:** The Auditorium has a stated seating capacity of 1000 individuals.
*   **Purpose:** It is regularly utilized for major events such as:
    *   Institute convocations
    *   Annual cultural festivals (e.g., Ragam)
    *   Annual technical festivals (e.g., Tathva)
    *   Guest lectures and distinguished speaker series
    *   Student body meetings and general assemblies
