# Open Air Theatre at NIT Calicut

## Overview
The Open Air Theatre (OAT) is a prominent amphitheatre located within the campus of the National Institute of Technology Calicut (NITC). It serves as a central venue for a wide array of cultural events, fests, and various institutional and student gatherings, providing a significant space for large assemblies.

## Details
The Open Air Theatre is designed as a large amphitheatre, providing a substantial space for audiences to gather for events.
*   **Capacity:** The theatre has a reported seating capacity of approximately 2000 individuals.
*   **Design:** Its amphitheatre design typically features tiered seating, which is characteristic of such venues, aiming to provide clear views of the stage for a large audience.
