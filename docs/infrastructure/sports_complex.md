# Sports Complex at NIT Calicut

## Overview
The Sports Complex at the National Institute of Technology Calicut (NIT Calicut) is a dedicated facility designed to promote physical education, sports, and recreational activities among its students, faculty, and staff. It serves as a central hub for athletic training, inter-collegiate competitions, and general fitness pursuits, aiming to foster a healthy lifestyle and competitive spirit within the institute community.

## Details
The management and operation of the Sports Complex facilities typically fall under the purview of the Physical Education Section of NIT Calicut. ## Facilities
The Sports Complex at NIT Calicut encompasses a range of indoor and outdoor facilities designed to support various sports and physical activities. The available facilities include:

*   **Outdoor Stadium:** Features a 400-meter athletics track and a football field, serving as the primary venue for track and field events and football matches.
*   **Indoor Sports Complex:** Houses multiple courts for badminton and facilities for table tennis. It also includes a gymnasium.
*   **Basketball Courts:** Multiple outdoor courts are available for basketball.
*   **Volleyball Courts:** Several outdoor courts are designated for volleyball.
*   **Tennis Courts:** Outdoor tennis courts are provided for practice and matches.
*   **Swimming Pool:** An Olympic-size swimming pool is available for aquatic sports and recreational swimming.
*   **Cricket Nets:** Dedicated practice nets for cricket are provided.
*   **Gymnasium:** A well-equipped gymnasium is available for strength training, cardio exercises, and general fitness. (Note: This may be part of the Indoor Sports Complex or a separate facility depending on specific campus layout and updates.)
